Modern Slang I Wish Would Go Away

Slang: "The Ick"

Definition: Used to refer to a sudden feeling of disgust or repulsion to a partner or someone you are attracted to.

Why I Don't Like It: It has been used to nit-pick character traits about other people, one source describes it as "irrational turn-offs." It is an easy way to demean people's character, and I have seen it been used in that way many times.
